MAKING THINGS HAPPEN

When I became your First Minister last year, I knew that we had some work to do. Voters across Wales were making it clear to me that you felt we had lost touch – that your Government needed to refocus.

That is why the first thing I did was to embark on a listening tour. I came to your high streets, community centres, schools, hospitals and more to hear from you about the things that you thought the Government should be doing. And in doing so, I came up with a list of four priorities – detailed down below – that you wanted the Government to focus on.

And then I got to work.

In the months since, my Government has been making things happen, delivering on those priorities, and below you can find everything we have done so far.

I've listened, I've heard. And now I'm making things happen.
